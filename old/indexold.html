About Us

“Sportrix” is the Sports Cell of Kalyani Government Engineering college, strongly recognizes the idea of "Sports for All" as access to sports is being considered the fundamental right to all which seems to preserve integrity of sports in the College. The College has registered its repute not only in academics, but also in the field of sports too.
